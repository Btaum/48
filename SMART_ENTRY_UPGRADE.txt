BOT11 Smart Entry Upgrade

Changes applied:
- Paper mode remains default.
- Entry order type remains LIMIT.
- Pullback execution is now ON by default.
- New Dash-style smart entry calculator added.
- Candidate limit prices are calculated from:
  - KN EMA5 pullback
  - KN EMA12 deeper pullback
  - last closed candle 50% midpoint
  - last closed candle 0.382 fib pullback
  - last closed candle 0.500 fib pullback
  - support/resistance
  - Market Memory cloud/line when available
- The bot chooses a value-area pullback instead of chasing current price.
- Pending smart-entry orders expire after 8 closed 5m candles or the existing pendingExpiryMinutes safety timeout.
- Pending limit fills are checked only using closed 5m candles after order placement.

Important:
- This does not guarantee wins. It is designed to reduce FOMO/market-chase entries and improve average entry quality.
- Keep paper mode on until you manually review results.
